Xiaomi's in-house XRing 01 SoC leaked

Xiaomi is creating its first processor, the XRing 01, with 3 nanometer technology, for use in future cell phones of the brand.

The new SoC features a 10-core Arm Cortex CPU plus 16-core Mali G925 GPU, positioning Xiaomi to compete with other major smartphone manufacturers who develop their own chips.

This move represents a significant step for Xiaomi in controlling more of its hardware ecosystem and potentially reducing dependency on third-party chip manufacturers.
